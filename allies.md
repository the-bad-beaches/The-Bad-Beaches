# List of ally characters

These are the characters that can be used by the player in the game to complete the missions. (Not NPCs)

I tried to include characters that should be found near the sea in our world.

1. Monkey Ranger
  - Creature: Monkey.
  - Class: Sniper
  - Abilities: Long range attacks, low HP, average damage and speed.
  - Late game buff: Shoots multiple (2) arrows at once?

2. Shell Knight
  - Creature: Turtle.
  - Class: Tank
  - Abilities: High HP, slow speed, melee attacks and low damage.
  - Late game buff: Reflects a portion of melee damage back to attacker.

3. Luma Tender
  - Creature: A gecko or iguana with light crystals embedded in its skin.
  - Class: Generator
  - Abilities: Generates light, does not assist in combat. Average HP.
  - Late game buff: Generates bursts of light on top of the passive regen.

4. Albatross Skystrike
  - Creature: A large albatross.
  - Class: AOE Helidrop (Air support)
  - Abilities: Drops once, does huge AOE damage and leaves. Does not participate in combat otherwise.
  - Late game buff: Increased AOE size and damage.

5. Komodo Ravager
  - Description: A komodo dragon (look pretty cool already TBH)
  - Class: Melee DPS
  - Abilities: High damage melee attacks, average HP and fast speed.
  - Late game buff: Does (%max HP) bleed damage over time on melee hits.

6. Dart Shaman
  - Creature: A poison dart frog.
  - Class: Debuffer
  - Abilities: Ranged attacks that apply poison debuff, low HP, low damage and high speed. Poison DOT is their main damage source instead of plain attacks.
  - Late game buff: Increased poison duration and slow+damage.

7. Light Priest
  - Creature: Sea otter?
  - Class: Healer
  - Abilities: Heals allies, low HP, no damage, average speed.
  - Only available in late game no extra buff.
